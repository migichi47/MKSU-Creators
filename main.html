About the Awards

The MKSU Creators Awards recognize outstanding achievements in various creative fields, including art, music, literature, and technology. These awards celebrate innovation, creativity, and excellence among creators worldwide.

Categories

  • Comedian of the Year
  • Dancer of the Year
  • Musician of the Year
  • Vlogger of the Year
  • Influencer of the Year

Voting

Any Machakos university student can vote for the best creators of their choice.

Award Ceremony

The award ceremony is held annually in a prestigious venue, where winners are announced and celebrated. The event includes performances, speeches, and networking opportunities for creators and industry professionals.
